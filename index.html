Cooking 👨‍🍳

I enjoy cooking! I'm not a great chef or even a good cooker, but I master my classics. And as a good recipe is worth all the talk, here is the recipe for my favorite dish: carbonara pasta!

  1. Cook the pasta in a large volume of salted boiling water.
  2. Slice the onions and brown them in the pan. As soon as they are golden brown, add the bacon.
  3. Prepare the "crème fraiche", eggs, salt and pepper in a salad bowl and mix.
  4. Remove the bacon from the heat as soon as they are golden and add them to the cream.
  5. Once the pasta is cooked al dente, drain it and add the cream. Return to the heat if the dish has cooled.
  6. Serve and enjoy your meal! You can also decorate your dish with mushrooms.
